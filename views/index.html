Frequently Asked Questions

What is a virtual phone number?
+
A virtual phone number is a real phone number that can receive SMS messages but isn't tied to a physical SIM card. It's perfect for verifying accounts without using your personal number.
What if I don't receive the OTP?
+
If no OTP is received within 20 minutes, you'll received an automatic full refund to your wallet. No questions asked.
Which payment methods are accepted?
+
We accept UPI, cryptocurrency (USDT, BTC, ETH), and other methods. Check the deposit page for currently available options.
Is it safe to use?
+
Yes. Your password is encrypted with bcrypt and never stored in plain text. Numbers are discarded after use. Orders are completely private to your account.
Can I use the same number again?
+
No — each number is single-use and discarded after your order. This is intentional to maximize privacy and ensure the number works for verification services.
